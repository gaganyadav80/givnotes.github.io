About givnotes

Givnotes is a notes taking application which is originated with the idea of having a functional yet powerful notes taking application but with a UI that is both pleasing to the eyes and also light on your phone by not making this app a resource hog.

Givnotes is still in it's beta stages as due to my work schedules it is not under heavy development. So, stay tunes and the final release will be in December.

It has not yet decided if this will be a commercial app or a always free app and about being open-source that will be deided with the above decision.
